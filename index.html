About Will

Hey, I’m Will. A lot a of people like to say they are creative problem solvers, but I legitimately am (well according to those personality assessments...?). I love to learn how things work and how we can make them better. After a decade in the nonprofit world working directly with people I’ve made the jump into the programming world. I’m passionate about solving problems, and dedicated to make a difference beyond the workplace.

My journey produced experiences in Wisconsin, Western New York, inner-city Chicago, and now the suburbs of Cleveland. I'm thankful how it's cultivated a natural ability to connect people of various age, race, and socioeconomic backgrounds. I always look forward to learn the culture, history, and style of the people around me. I have a strong desire to live a life that goes beyond the American dream. My dreams are bigger than that. I want to be an excellent worker and even better human. I’m excited about people who are making a difference in the world. Checkout one of my favorite organizations: Feed My Starving Children
